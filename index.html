Honorarium Desktop Client

A secure anonymous decentralized payment processor aiming at simplifying trades in cryptocurrency while offering full control over your funds and anonymity thanks to true multisig wallets and the privacy-focused Monero cryptocurrency.

Download Learn More

Simple, Clean and Intuitive Interface

Easily keep track of payments, outgoing and incoming transactions and pay or refund with one click. The whole multisig wallet creation happens under the hood, no interaction is needed from the user; private keys never leave your disk, stay secure with our true 2:3 moderated escrow payments.

Fully Secure

A multisignature wallet ensures no one of the three parties involved (customer, merchant and moderator) can move funds from the wallet without at least another peer agreeing. In this way, exit scams can be prevented while disputes can easily be handled by an independent objective third party.

Totally Anonymous

Internally, we use Monero (XMR), an untraceable privacy-focused cryptocurrency that breaks the chain of payments made in other crypto whilst preserving the financial value of transactions. Moreover, via the advanced settings page it is possible to configure a SOCKS proxy (like Tor) to mask the originating IP

Cross-Platform Compatible

Thanks to the power of web technologies, it runs on Windows, Mac and Linux (ubuntu and debian-based distros) preserving the same functionality. Wallets and log files are saved in the user root directory. The app already packs the necessary Monero executables to work, just login and you're ready to go.

Supported Currencies

Using an external decentralized exchange, we provide instant cryptocurrency conversion so that the customer can pay in any of the supported crypto while the internal transaction is carried out in Monero in order to break the chain and guarantee the highest security standard for all parties involved.
